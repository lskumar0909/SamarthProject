About Lego

Lego is an interactive toy for all ages and is very fun to play with. Lego is very
popular all around the world and you can make whatever you want. You can also buy lego sets which have a lego structure and some lego figures.

The lego group was founded in 1932 by Ola Kirk Kristiansen. It started off as a normal lego company
and became one of the most famous toy manufacturers in the world. Amazingly, LEGO produces 318 plastic tires a year.
When the Lego Minifigure was introduced in 1975, the company wanted the builders to project their imaginations onto faceless figures. A team of ambitious builders decided to build a 112-foot tower out of Lego bricks.
They were put into a world records book. The name lego came from the first two letters of the Danish
word Leg and Godt meaning play well. Did you know that there are around 400 billion
Lego bricks around the world? If you were to stack all of these Legos together, this tower would 2,386,065 miles (3,839,999 kilometers) tall. This
meaning that it would stretch ten times farther than the distance to the moon. A British man named Hilary Fisher Page (1904-1957) invented a self-locking brick that was a predecessor to Lego. The Lego bricks were based on these.
The first Lego blocks were patented in 1949.
